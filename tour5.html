Discover the Magic of Wadi Rum with Our 6 Hours Jeep Tour

Overview

Join us on a private full-day Jeep tour led by local guides, where you’ll explore dramatic landscapes, iconic rock formations, and hidden canyons in the heart of Wadi Rum.

Seventy dinars for two people, including food

Tour Itinerary

Morning

Start from the village, visit Lawrence Spring and the Red Sand Dunes.

Midday

Explore Khazali Canyon, enjoy a traditional Bedouin lunch in the shade.

Evening

💰 6-Hour Jeep Tour – Full Experience

Embark on an unforgettable 6-hour desert adventure through the majestic landscapes of Wadi Rum for only 70 Jordanian Dinars per tour. The price covers up to 6 people and includes a delicious traditional lunch served in the mountains, surrounded by stunning views and Bedouin hospitality.

📍 Tour Highlights:

  • 🏜️ Explore the famous colored sands of Wadi Rum and capture breathtaking photos
  • 🎬 Visit the movie area where many Hollywood films were shot
  • 🧭 Walk through the legendary Siq Al Barah canyon
  • 🧗‍♂️ See popular rock climbing spots along the way
  • ☕ Stop at a traditional Bedouin bazaar to enjoy fresh tea and cultural ambiance
  • 🪨 Discover the iconic Mushroom Rock formation
  • ⛰️ Climb Umm Al Furuth Tower and admire panoramic views including Burda Mountain
  • 🌅 Finish your tour by watching a magical desert sunset before returning to camp

This tour is perfect for families, groups, or solo travelers looking to experience the best of Wadi Rum in just half a day.

Details

  • Check-in at 9:30 AM
  • Return after sunset
  • Max 6 guests per Jeep
  • Easy to moderate terrain
  • Optional: Camel ride, Overnight camp
