Who We Are

Bronze Mountain Camp is your gateway to an authentic and unforgettable desert camping experience in the heart of Wadi Rum. Established with a passion for preserving Bedouin traditions and offering exceptional hospitality, we blend comfort and adventure under the spectacular desert skies.

Our dedicated team is committed to creating memorable moments that connect you with the beauty and serenity of the desert.

How We Treat Our Guests

At Bronze Mountain Camp, our guests are at the center of everything we do. We believe that a great camping experience is built on:

  • Personalized Service: From the moment you inquire to your last night under the stars, we tailor every detail to your preferences.
  • Authentic Hospitality: We welcome you with Bedouin warmth, sharing stories, traditional meals, and cultural insights.
  • Safety and Comfort: Our camps are equipped with clean facilities, quality bedding, and experienced guides to ensure your peace of mind.
  • Respect for Nature: We prioritize eco-friendly practices to preserve Wadi Rum's natural beauty for generations to come.
  • Continuous Support: Our team is available 24/7 for any questions or assistance before, during, and after your stay.

Our Commitment

We strive to deliver a seamless experience, combining adventure, culture, and relaxation. By choosing Bronze Mountain Camp, you are not only booking a trip but becoming part of a story woven by the desert winds and ancient sands.

We invite you to explore, dream, and create lasting memories with us.

Discover the Desert & Our Camp

Nestled amidst the vast golden dunes, our desert camp blends seamlessly with the natural beauty of the landscape. Surrounded by towering sandstone cliffs and endless skies, the camp features traditional Bedouin-style tents equipped with modern comforts, offering you an authentic yet cozy experience. Whether you relax under the starry desert night or embark on adventurous camel rides, the breathtaking views and peaceful ambiance make every moment unforgettable.

About Our Desert Camp

Our desert camp offers a unique escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Nestled in the heart of the desert, we provide cozy tents equipped with all essentials for a comfortable stay, delicious traditional meals, and exciting activities like camel rides, stargazing, and cultural performances. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, our camp is designed to connect you deeply with nature while respecting and preserving the environment. Join us to experience authentic desert life and create unforgettable memories under the vast, star-filled sky.

The Beauty of Our Desert and Camping Journey

The beauty of our desert lies not only in its golden sands and shining sun but also in the breathtaking tranquility it offers. Our camping journey takes you on a trip through the enchanting desert nature, amidst shifting dunes kissed by the gentle breeze and under the peaceful glow of the moonlit sky. During the trip, you can enjoy camel rides, listen to Bedouin stories, and feel the calmness away from the city's noise. It's a complete experience that lets you live the spirit of the desert in every detail.
