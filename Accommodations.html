Options for Your Overnight Stay in Wadi Rum

Sleep Beneath the Stars or Stay Cozy at Camp

We offer two unique overnight experiences in the heart of Wadi Rum. Whether you want the comfort of a Bedouin-style camp or the magic of sleeping in a remote natural cave, you’ll find your perfect desert adventure with us.

Wadi Rum Base Camp

Our main camp combines tradition and comfort. You’ll enjoy clean tents, shared bathrooms, delicious Bedouin meals, and evenings around the campfire under the stars.

Desert Cave Experience

For adventurous souls, our open-air cave offers the purest way to experience the desert night. No lights, no walls — just you, the stars, and nature at its finest.

About Bronze Mountains Camp

At Bronze Mountains Camp, we’re proud of our Bedouin heritage and the experiences we offer our guests. Each traveler is treated with genuine hospitality, honesty, and respect. We go above and beyond to make your stay memorable — whether it’s preparing special meals, tailoring private tours, or simply sharing stories by the fire. Our team speaks multiple languages, understands different cultural expectations, and treats every guest like family. Your comfort, safety, and joy are our top priorities.
