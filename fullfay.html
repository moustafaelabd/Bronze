Embark on the Ultimate Wadi Rum Adventure with Our Full-Day Jeep Tour

Tour Highlights

Experience the vast beauty of Wadi Rum with a private jeep tour guided by local experts. Enjoy stunning desert landscapes, traditional Bedouin lunch, and unforgettable photo stops at iconic locations.

Price: 70 JOD for two persons

Tour Itinerary

Start of Day

Begin your journey from the village and head to Lawrence Spring, a scenic spot with sweeping views and historical importance.

Midday Adventures

Traverse the vast sand dunes and canyons, stop by Khazali Canyon, and savor a freshly prepared lunch in a shaded desert spot.

Sunset Moments

Climb Um Fruth Rock Bridge and conclude your adventure with a mesmerizing sunset from a peaceful desert viewpoint.

Additional Info

  • Check-in: 9:30 AM
  • Departure: 30–45 minutes after sunset
  • Max Group Size: 6 people per shared tour
  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Add-ons: Camel Ride | Overnight Stay
